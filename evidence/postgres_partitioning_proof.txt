Both use the (ticker, date) index, so at our modest data volume the timing difference is small -- the real benefit of partitioning shows up at much larger scale (millions of rows per year), where the planner skips reading entire years' worth of data pages instead of just filtering rows within one table. We're including this honest comparison rather than overstating the speedup at our current data size.
